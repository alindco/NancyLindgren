Funerals & Memorials:  

Facing the decision to create a farewell ceremony for a loved one can be a tender time, let ( Reverend )Nancy assist you in planning the funeral ceremony.  She can help you to create the farewell service that reflects the feelings and beliefs of your departed loved one. 

With the help of your family and friends, Rev. Lindgren will carefully script a tribute of the character and life of your dearly departed.  This tribute will be a celebration of his/her life and your emotions towards your loved one.  Each funeral ceremony is unique to capture the very essence of his/her lasting spirit.  

Saying farewell to the flesh is just the beginning of saying goodbye. Releasing his/her soul will be your way of letting go and this will make the journey of your dearly departed easier. A customized service can be nondenominational, interfaith, or civil.

Nancy is an officiant who cares about what you are going through and cares about the journey of your loved one.
